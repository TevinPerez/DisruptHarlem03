G A N T Z

This website contains two site pages of characters from the manga "GANTZ".

Former childhood friends, separated by time, reunited by death; they come to realize what the world is coming to, and what they have to do about it.

After dying trying to save an old homeless drunk that fell onto train tracks, Kei Kurono and Masaru Kato awake in a lonely locked apartment only housing a large black sphere. They come to find out that everyone that appears in that room has died and that they now belong to a "game" of alien hunt. The sphere then opens, revealing suitcases (with outfits) and weapons, which don't seem to work at the time. They soon realize how important their missions are.
