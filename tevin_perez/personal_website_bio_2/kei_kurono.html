Kei Kurono in GANTZ

Kei Kurono (Kurono) is a high-school nobody that gets thown into a world of chaos when he dies suddenly trying to save an old man. He and his former childhood friend, Masaru Kato (Kato), learn that there can be life after death, but that life..is just a game.
